Jaco also offers a lively and welcoming surf culture

The best time to visit for ideal surfing conditions

While Jaco has consistent waves throughout the year, the weather can vary depending on the season, which can affect the surf conditions. The best weather to surf in Jaco is during the dry season, which runs from December to April. During this time, the weather is warm, sunny, and dry,

  • Offshore winds that create clean and consistent waves.
  • This is the peak season for surfing in Jaco.
  • a great time to take a Javi surf lesson and improve your skills on the waves.

During the rainy season, which runs from May to November, the weather in Jaco is more unpredictable, with occasional rain showers and thunderstorms. The waves can still be good during this time, but the wind and rain can make for less than ideal surfing conditions. However, the rainy season is a great time to visit if you're looking for fewer crowds and lower prices.
